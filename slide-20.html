The Event Study Regression

$$Y_{it} = \alpha_i + \gamma_t + \sum_{\substack{k \neq -1}} \color{#0072B2}{\beta_k} \cdot D_i \cdot \mathbf{1}\!\left(t - t^*_i = k\right) + u_{it}$$

\(\alpha_i\) = unit fixed effects (absorb time-invariant differences between groups)
\(\gamma_t\) = time fixed effects (absorb common shocks affecting all units)
\(D_i\) = treatment indicator (1 if unit i is ever treated)
\(\mathbf{1}(t - t^*_i = k)\) = indicator for being \(k\) periods from treatment
\(\beta_k\) = the treatment effect at relative time \(k\) (what we plot!)
\(k \neq -1\) we exclude one period as the reference (normalization)
