Returns to Audits Vary by Income

How much revenue does $1 of audit spending generate?

Income Group Revenue per $1 Spent
Bottom 50% $0.96
50th-90th percentile $2.17
90th-99th percentile $12.48
Top 1% $4.25
Top 0.1% $6.29

Key finding: Auditing low-income taxpayers actually loses money—the IRS spends more on the audit than it recovers.
