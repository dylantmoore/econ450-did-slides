Case Study: Trade Adjustment Assistance

Hyman (2018) — "Can Displaced Labor Be Retrained?"

The Policy Question: Does the federal Trade Adjustment Assistance (TAA) program help displaced manufacturing workers?

What is TAA?

  • Federal program (since 1962) for workers who lose jobs due to trade competition
  • Benefits: Up to 2 years of job retraining + extended unemployment insurance
  • ~$1 billion/year; 230,000+ workers enrolled in 2010

The Identification Challenge: Workers who receive TAA are selected—their layoffs were caused by trade. Are they comparable to workers denied TAA?
