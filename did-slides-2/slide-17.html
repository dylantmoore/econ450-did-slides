How Audits Compare to Tax Rate Changes

Tax Rate Changes

MVPF: 1.0 → 2.0+

Low income → High income

vs

Tax Audits

MVPF: 1.30 → 1.15

Low income → High income

The Key Insight

MVPFs of tax/transfer policies increase with income
MVPFs of audits decrease with income

Policy implication: Audits on high-income taxpayers raise revenue at lower welfare cost than increasing their tax rates.

Why? Raising tax rates on high earners creates big behavioral distortions (work less, shift income, relocate). Audits don't change incentives—they just enforce existing rules.
