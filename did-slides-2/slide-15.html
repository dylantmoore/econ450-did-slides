The Marginal Value of Public Funds (MVPF)

The MVPF Formula

MVPF = Willingness to Pay to Avoid Policy
Net Cost to Government

For audits specifically:

MVPF = (R + B) / (R - C)

where R = revenue raised, C = audit cost, B = burden on taxpayer

Lower MVPF = Better
More efficient way to raise revenue

Benchmark: MVPF = 1
A "lump-sum" tax with no distortions

Intuition: MVPF captures the welfare cost per dollar of revenue. It tells us how much society "pays" (in burden + foregone revenue) for each dollar the government actually nets.
